In October 2025, I had the honor of speaking at Curicaca Festival, an event promoted by Concentro and the Brazilian Industrial Development Agency (ABDI), recognized as the largest youth entrepreneurship summit in the Brazilian Midwest. Under the theme "How to Utilize Artificial Intelligence to Create Efficient Solutions," I addressed how AI can be applied to optimize internal processes, support strategic decision-making, and enhance the quality of client delivery.

The core thesis of the presentation argues that technology should act as an operational "right hand," providing the "breathing room" necessary for human talent to focus on irreplaceable skills: creativity and critical thinking. I highlighted that AI adoption is not a "silver bullet" and requires organizational maturity, data governance (Garbage In, Garbage Out), and conscious investment to generate real results.
